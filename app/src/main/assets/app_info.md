# Ezek's Translator version a1.0.0

## Current Features

* Online translation through Lingva (I'll add DeepL later)
* Local translation through transformers Models in CTranslate2
* 'Lite' models (Quantised versions of the OPUS-MT Models)
* 'Full' models (OPUS-MT or Quick-MT Models with BLEU score of 32-48)
* Ability to download & delete these models at any time

## Known Issues

* Camera Translator is still not implemented & crashes on attempting to take a picture (Will be added in future update)
* Phrasebook not implemented (Will be added in future update)
* Settings page doesn't exist
